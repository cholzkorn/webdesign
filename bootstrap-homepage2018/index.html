about

Hello! This homepage is a way of showcasing my interests and projects. Working hard for the things I care about and sharing some of this work with you is something I enjoy very much.

data science

I was almost done with my bachelor's degree in economics, when I took a course about statistical programming. This is when I fell in love with data science as a discipline. Extracting information and making predictions gives me the magnificent feeling of discovering something nobody has discovered before.

Although I am open for using whatever gets the job done, the technologies below are the ones I am most familiar with.

My work in this field can be found in my data science github repository.

economics

Since I am deeply interested in economics, business and statistics, I started a bachelor's degree in economics at the University of Vienna, which I finished in 2016. As of right now, I am writing a book for undergraduate students about pluralist economics as my master's thesis. The economics and econometrics papers I have written can be found here.

media design

In 2007 (when I was 14) I applied to business school, only because it offered a branch in Business Technologies. There I mostly learned the basics of software development and media design. I used these skills in our graduation projects as well, as in designing this homepage, which I built with the Bootstrap HTML, CSS and JavaScript library.


teaching

In 2016 and 2017 I had the privilege of teaching Microeconomics to Business Technologies students at TU Vienna in the form of exercise classes. My tasks included giving instructions in class as well as correcting exams at the end of the semester. As of now, I am studying Business Education at WU Vienna.
